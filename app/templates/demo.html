How to Use the Demo

  1. Start a Conversation: Click "Try the Demo" to open the WhatsApp-style chat interface
  2. Choose Job Type: Select from Photography, Videography, Audio, or Other services
  3. Provide Details: Follow the conversation to provide job date, duration, location, and budget
  4. Contact Information: Enter your name, phone number, and email address
  5. Review & Confirm: Review the booking summary and confirm your booking
  6. CRM Integration: Watch as the system creates contacts, calendar events, and tasks in Zoho CRM

Note: This is a proof-of-concept demo. The Zoho CRM integration is simulated with realistic delays and data generation. In production, this would connect to your actual Zoho CRM instance.
